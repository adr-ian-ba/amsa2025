IMSTC 2025

Indonesian Medical Students' Training and Competition (IMSTC), organized by AMSA-Universitas Indonesia (AMSA-UI), is a premier platform that empowers future medical professionals through a blend of competition, collaboration, and education. Designed to foster innovation, critical thinking, and leadership, IMSTC 2025 brings together students from across Indonesia to explore groundbreaking ideas and advance their skills in the medical field. More than just a competition, this event inspires unity, learning, and a shared commitment to excellence in healthcare.

IMSTC 2025

We have chosen to expand upon our theme, REBORN, in order to unravel the rising problems we may encounter within the field of obstetrics and gynecology and impart to participants on the topic. With the growing lack of apprehension towards the current, escalating problems, we wish that REBORN will be able to share awareness whilst simultaneously hoisting the spirit of competition and upraise a sense of togetherness.

Competition

Scientific Poster

Scientific posters can be a powerful way to showcase data in a scientific meeting. Poster sessions provide a more relaxed session compared to presentations. These posters provide means of spreading awareness to medical professionals and researchers alike.

Scientific Paper

The scientific paper competition provides a more formal approach in spreading awareness. These papers can share crucial information to researchers and medical professionals alike and spread awareness regarding the problems faced in the field of obstetrics and gynecology.

Public Poster

The Public poster competition provides means of spreading awareness to the public regarding the rising problems we face in the obstetrics and gynecology field in the form of posters that can be shared to the public as a form of social act from medical students all across Indonesia.

Photography

The photography competition allows participants to open the window into the field of obstetrics and gynecology whilst simultaneously allowing them to pour in their creativity and view regarding the field in the form of photography.

Videography

The videography competition allows participants to open the window into the field of obstetrics and gynecology whilst simultaneously allowing them to pour in their creativity and view regarding the field in the form of videography.

FAQ

Who can take part in IMSTC 2025?

IMSTC 2025: REBORN is open for all AMSA members all over indonesia

What competitions are available for participation in IMSTC 2025?

IMSTC 2025: REBORN has arranged a series of competitions, including scientific poster, scientific paper, public poster, photography, and videography.

When and where will IMSTC 2025 be held?

IMSTC 2025: REBORN will be held in several locations across Jakarta and the UI Depok complex.
